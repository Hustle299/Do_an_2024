Welcome to the Hand Measurement System

This application uses state-of-the-art computer vision techniques to provide accurate hand measurements. Whether you need it for research, design, or engineering, our system ensures precision and usability.

Start by downloading the CAD file and the calibration pattern, then follow the steps below to measure your hand.

Download CAD File Download Chessboard

How It Works

  1. Step 0: Download the CAD file and print it using a 3D printer.
  2. Step 1: Print the calibration chessboard on A4 paper.
  3. Step 2: Take 10 photos of the chessboard from different angles and upload them for calibration.
  4. Step 3: Upload images of your hand for measurement.
  5. Step 4: View detailed measurements on the Library page.
Tip: For the best results, use a well-lit area and ensure the images are clear and focused.
